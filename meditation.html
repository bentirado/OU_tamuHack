Pre-Flight Calm

A simple breathing exercise to prepare for your journey:

1. Inhale for 4 seconds: Breathe in slowly and deeply through your nose.

2. Hold for 7 seconds: Hold your breath gently, allowing the air to settle in your lungs.

3. Exhale for 8 seconds: Slowly exhale through your mouth, making a soft whooshing sound.

Repeat this cycle 3-4 times to help relax and center your mind before your flight.

In-Flight Meditation

A simple meditation to stay centered and relaxed during your flight:

1. Find a comfortable position: Sit back in your seat, rest your hands on your lap, and gently close your eyes.

2. Focus on your breath: Pay attention to the natural rhythm of your breathing. Don’t force it—just observe the sensation of air entering and leaving your body.

3. Let thoughts pass by: If your mind begins to wander, acknowledge the thoughts without judgment, and gently bring your focus back to your breath.

4. Ground yourself: Feel the connection between your feet and the floor, and the back of your body against the seat. This helps you feel grounded and present.

Practice this for 5-10 minutes to help reduce stress and maintain a calm mindset during your flight.

Anxiety Relief

A quick technique for relief during moments of anxiety:

1. Focus on your breathing: Close your eyes and take slow, deep breaths. Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, then exhale for 4 seconds.

2. Ground yourself: Place your hands on your legs and feel the sensation of your feet pressing into the floor. Notice your surroundings and use your senses to ground yourself in the present moment.

3. Repeat a calming word: Silently repeat a calming word, like "peace" or "calm," as you breathe in and out.

Try this technique whenever you feel your anxiety start to rise, allowing yourself to regain composure and calm.
